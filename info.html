Nissan 240SX

When Vin first picked up this dusty old Nissan 240sx, it had seen better days. Much... much better days. Skyline GTR R32

We took a Skyline GTR shell and transformed it into a 645hp, ethanol breathing, top mount twins. (not really but you get to see it happen that fast). From the Blitz Twin Turbo kit to the bronze TE37's, this GTR does not disappoint. Completely built and restored at Driver Motorsports, no detail was overlooked.

Every part on this car was polished, painted or powder coated! She pulls hard on 100% E85 with 645WHP recorded on the dyno! To hold this power, she has a NISMO Coppermix twin-plate clutch and some sticky Toyo Tires. To address handling, we equipped her with some Fortune Auto coilovers.

If you haven't driven a car with Fortune Auto's, you need to. Many custom parts were fabricated in house. Just scope that custom intake box and turbo piping. From this inside out this car is simply sublime. We have put a lot of blood, sweat, and tears into this car. We have had several customers buy a car from us and take it to a car show and win and this car is a show stopper!

Toyota Supra MK4

Restoring and rebuilding my 1995 Field Find Toyota Supra in 10 minutes! This car build/restoration took about 2 years from start to finish You can watch all the videos on this build start to finish on my youtube channel MotionAutoTv
